About the Projects

BMERIT Adaptive Handcycle

Neuro-rehab for upper extremities.

This project from BMERIT (Biomedical Engineering Research and Innovation Team)'s mechanical team is an adaptable and inclusive theraputical device to assist in neurorehabilitation by improving the user's upper extremity mobility and cardiovascular endurance. It uses a bicycle trainer as the handcycle, which is mounted onto a frame made with off the shelf aluminum T-slot extrusions and controlled with a linear actuator.

BMERIT Cyberstride

Adding modules on walkers to provide quantitative data to physiotherapists.

This multidisciplinary project from BMERIT (Biomedical Engineering Research and Innovation Team)'s software, electrical, and mechanical teams focuses on improving the feedback that physiotherapists recieve from their patients by adding various sensor modules onto a standard walker to record various patient metrics. These metrics are then displayed to the physiotherapist in an easy to read format and the data from a session can be saved. The modules themselves are intended to fit on a variety of walkers.

ReVOLT EV Motorcycle

Converting an old gas motorcycle to be an EV.

The focus of this project is to convert an older gas-powered motorcycle into an electric vehicle.
