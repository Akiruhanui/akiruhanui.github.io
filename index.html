About Me

Hello! My name's Qasim. I'm currently in my undergrad at the University of Calgary, where I'm studying Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Biomedical Engineering.

Ever since I was young, I always loved to make things. I spent a lot of time building with LEGO, even into the start of university. It was from this hobby, and a friend of mine telling me that I should be a mechanical engineer back in 2017 (when I was in grade 7), that drove me to pursuing this major.


Since then, I've had the chance to learn many things throughout my degree, and I have been blessed with the opportunity to have done an undergraduate research term and to join some technical clubs. These experiences have definitely given more value to what I have learned, and have been an important part of my university experience.


In addition to LEGO and engineering, I also like taking random landscape and architecture (?) photographs for fun. This was definitely influenced by a photography class I took in high school.
